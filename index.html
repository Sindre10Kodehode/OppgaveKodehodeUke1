Who Am I?

I'm the creative type. I'm a musician by trade, and a future programmer. Music is my passion, and it does pay some bills, but not nearly enough to keep me financially afloat. Because of this I have been looking into other options. Throughout my entire life I have had a fascination with the art of programming. That is exactly how I think of it; an artform. I've always wanted to learn it, but never had the time to get into it, until now.

The past 4 years I have been playing actively in several bands. I've played almost every single stage in the Porsgrunn - Skien area, and everywhere else from Kristiansand to Oslo, and in between. So far I've played mostly covers with "Loose Ends" and "Unfinished Business". I'm working on a lot of original songs, but I'm too much of a perfetionist to have finished any of them yet.
